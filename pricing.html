Common questions

What happens during the free trial?

Ava is fully configured for your business and starts answering calls immediately. You get 7 full days to see the difference before we charge anything. No credit card required to start.

Can I change plans?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade any time. When you upgrade, the new AI employees start working that day. When you downgrade, it takes effect at the next billing cycle.

How does Ava learn my business?

When you sign up, you tell Ava about your services, pricing, hours, and how you want calls handled. Ava learns from every call and improves weekly. You can update her knowledge any time from your dashboard.

What happens if I go over my call limit?

We'll notify you at 80% of your limit. You can upgrade mid-month or top up for $0.15 per additional call. We will never cut off your calls without warning.

What integrations does Aria support?

Aria integrates with most major booking, CRM, and accounting tools including ServiceM8, Jobber, Cliniko, Mindbody, Xero, and MYOB. More integrations are added monthly. Email us if you need something specific.

Is my data safe?

All data is stored in Australia. We never share your business data or customer information with third parties. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We are compliant with the Australian Privacy Act.
