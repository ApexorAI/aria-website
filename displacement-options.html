What we believe

You did not do anything wrong. The technology changed faster than the economy had time to adjust. That is not your failure — that is a systemic shift that no individual can prepare for.

We built part of that shift. Aria makes it easier for businesses to replace human roles with AI. We do that knowingly and we list every role publicly on our website because we believe in transparency about consequences.

But the fact that we are honest about it does not reduce the real impact on real people. Losing income is serious. Losing a career path you invested years in is serious. The fact that it was economically inevitable does not make it less hard.

So we are doing what we can. Which is not enough. But it is real, and it is permanent, and it will get bigger as Aria grows.

If you are reading this page, we see you. And we have not forgotten that the product that makes Aria work is the same product that changed your situation.
